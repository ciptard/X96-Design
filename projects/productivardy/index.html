About the Project

ProductiVardy is a weekly podcast hosted by Mike Vardy that will explore the productivity realm, lifehacking and much more. In addition, it’ll offer regular segments that go beyond interviewing those that are known in productivity circles, such as tips, tricks, product reviews and the occasional, unexpected audible treat for listeners. Put all of that together and you’ll see (and hear) that ProductiVardy is the productivity podcast with personality.
